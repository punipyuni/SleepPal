# SleepPal
an innovative mobile application designed to enhance sleep quality by providing users with comprehensive tools and insights to establish and maintain healthy sleep routines.

## Devices
* Android (Main Focus)
* iOS (Later or Never)

## Features
* **Sleep Tracking:** Collect data on sleep duration, quality, and disruptions using smart watch or manual input. 
* **Behavioral Analysis:** Analyze lifestyle factors such as diet, exercise, caffeine intake, and screen time before bed.
* **SleepGPT:** Suggest users to develop healthy sleep habits.
* **Relaxing or ASMR sound:** To relieve stress and anxiety.
* **Forum Section:** For the community to share and discussed their tips and tricks for better sleep
* **Sleeptime:** When the set sleep time is due, the app will block users from opening other
* **Predictive Analytics:** Use statistical modeling or machine learning algorithms and Correlation Analysis for Behavioral analysis to predict how certain behaviors may impact sleep quality.
